Instructions

The Basics:

  • Left click on the canvas to color one pixel.
  • Hold and drag after left clicking to color more.
  • Right click on the canvas to erase one pixel.
  • Click on the Button Box, Color Palette, or Pika Artist image
    and hold and drag the click to move them around.

Helpful Features:

  • Click on the "Clear" button, to bring the canvas back to the default.
  • Click on the "Save" button to save your work.
  • Click on the "Load Save" button to load your saved work back onto the canvas.
  • If you run into any problems refreshing the browser resets most things
  • Change the height and width values in the button box and click submit to change the size of the canvas.
    There is a minimum of 1 pixel to a max of up to 50.
  • You can zoom out on the browser if you need to see more.
  • Click on the ↶ or ↷ buttons to undo or redo a step.

Color features:

  • Click on a colored circle in the color palette to change colors.
  • Click on the "Colors" button and pick a color with the color picker
    or by typing in a hex-code, then click the "add color" button to
    add your chosen color to the color palette. You can add up to 29 additional colors.
  • Click on the "" button, to fill the canvas with one color.

Image features:

  • Click on the Link or Pika portraits to load an image onto the canvas.
  • Click on the "Load image" button to load an image onto the left blank portrait for inspiration.
  • Click on the "Add Image" button and choose a file or type in an image's link to add an image to the canvas.
    Note that the canvas has to be clear in order to see the image.
  • Click on the "Print Image" button to generate an image copy of your canvas.
